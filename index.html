Bio

I am Michela Parma, a web developer and digital humanist focused on designing tailored digital experiences. I work where creativity meets academic precision: designing websites and digital identities that are built from scratch, carefully structured, and shaped around the needs of each project.

With a background in history and digital humanities, I approach the web as both a design space and a research environment. I combine visual sensitivity — layout, typography, color — with analytical thinking and content structure.

My work is a slow process in the best sense: from concept to design to development, every step is intentional. No templates, no shortcuts — just thoughtful, handcrafted websites.
